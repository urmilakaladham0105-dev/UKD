About Urmila Kaladham

Urmila Kaladham is a creative academy dedicated to nurturing talent in Music, Dance, Art and Craft. Our mission is to provide quality artistic education in a positive and inspiring environment.

We believe that every child possesses unique creative abilities. Through proper guidance, practice and performance opportunities, we help students develop confidence, discipline and artistic excellence.

Our Courses

🎵 Music

Vocal music training including Classical, Bhajan, Devotional and Light Music.

💃 Dance

Classical and creative dance training for students of different age groups.

🎨 Art & Craft

Drawing, painting, sketching and creative craft activities for young learners.
